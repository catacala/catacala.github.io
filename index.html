Introducing Tungoo

Tungoo is a seamless vertical adventure starting from the ground and ending at the edge of space.

A seamless environment

Navigate through Tungoo's world with tilt controls and a multi-touch layout that will keep you honed in on the action.

Fun for all ages

Enjoy lip-smacking good collectibles, high flying boosts, bubble bursting excitement and so much more.

All new experience

Take control of Tungoo and battle your way to space to rescue your friends from their alien abductors in this bubble bursting vertical platformer.

Tungoo features a full in-game marketplace

Features

  • Create awesome new looks with over 100 pieces of gear to improve your stats and your look
  • Over 190 challenges to complete with in-game rewards
  • Sell what you collect for in-game BugBucks
